Voltaic Benchmarks

Assess your aim, track progress, participate in the leaderboards

About

The benchmarks are built as a set of scenarios, designed to assess your aim across various categories. There are several difficulty levels, ranging from
Novice
to
Advanced
.

Rules

Only the latest versions of each benchmark are eligible for the leaderboards and official VT ranks.
Cheating will result in a ban.
Use an FOV of 103 or higher. Do not pause mid-run.

Ranks

Upon reaching a rank in one of the latest benchmarks, you can claim it on the VT Discord by using
/getrank
in #commands.
It will also appear on your profile on the app.

Leaderboards

After earning some energy, you will be placed on the leaderboards for the benchmark.
The leaderboards are updated once a day, so they will always lag behind your latest scores.
